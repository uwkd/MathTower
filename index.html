About

I build ads that feel like games.

I work at the intersection of game design psychology and performance marketing — using IQ traps, satisfying mechanics, and micro-interactions to maximize engagement and CTR.

How I Work

From Brief → Banger Ad

01
🎯
Analyze the Game
Study the original game's core loop, art style, and monetization goal. Identify the single best mechanic to showcase.
02
🧠
Design the Trap
Engineer the IQ trap — the moment where the player *thinks* they can win, fails, and wants to download to try again.
03
⚡
Build & Optimize
Code it lean. No asset bloat, Tween-only animations, distance-based collision. Target: under 500KB, under 3s load.

Time the CTA button perfectly. Add heartbeat animation. Make the Download button the most satisfying tap in the ad.
